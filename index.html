About Me

Hi, I’m Hannah Stanley, a biomedical engineering student at the Georgia Institute of Technology! While I’m still exploring my specific interests within this diverse field, I’ve had the opportunity to refine my CAD skills during a co-op with Johnson & Johnson MedTech. Since then, I’ve brought these skills into my personal life by designing and creating miniature models, which I’m excited to share with you all.

Check out my projects below! You can download the STL files for my models, and if you’d like access to the SolidWorks files for customization or want to learn how I created them, feel free to reach out. You can email me at hstanley8@gatech.edu or connect with me on LinkedIn to send a message.
